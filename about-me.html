About Me

How I Got Started With Anime & Manga

I got started with anime I would like to say around the age of 5. This was way before I even knew what anime was, but the shows I watched were mainly Naruto, Yugioh, and Dragonball on this Cartoon network called Toonami it is a very old network that no longer exists but in the early 2000s that’s where anime would be streamed, so every Saturday afternoon I would watch either the newest episode of the three said shows every single week and I loved every second of it.

Now fast forward to around 2018 when I started to watch anime again because I stopped for a while due to certain circumstances, but I started back up in 2018 with my first series called one punch man I was watching it with my friend at the time and he mentioned it being an anime and I asked “What is anime exactly?” and he told me that it was basically cartoons made in japan and I proceeded to name some of the shows that I used to watch and he told me that those were anime’s so I was shocked to know that I have been watching anime all these years. After a little while I would like to say about 2 years ago when the pandemic started and I went to college that’s really when my anime watching was reignited.

I watched all of Naruto as a whole including it’s sequel which I did not know about until my friend showed me, so I was super excited to watch it and it later became my favorite anime of all time still is, but I do not consider it the best.

Now with manga I got introduced to it very late on in my life actually just last year I found out about manga and I started collecting them, because I myself personally like to read the physical copies I think it makes you more immersed into the story.

When I was living up in Tampa, Florida my collection of manga was decently big, but compared to it now is like a night and day difference.

That’s basically how I got started with anime and manga I’m really happy I discovered it, because it genuinely brings me a lot of joy and happiness whenever I’m experiencing a new series for the first time.
